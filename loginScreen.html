Welcome to Ecomeal a platform that helps reduce food waste

by connecting people who have extra food with those who need it. Together, we can save meals, help others, and protect the environment.

Every day, tons of perfectly good food are thrown away. Our mission is to give that food a second life.
Whether you’re a restaurant, bakery, home cook, or just someone with leftovers — you can share it here instead of throwing it away.
